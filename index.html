Stone, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock 

Create a program that calculates who wins the most games in rock, paper, scissors, lizard, spock.

- The result can be: "Player 1", "Player 2", "Tie"
                        
- The function receives a list containing pairs, representing each move.
                        
- The pair may contain combinations of "" (stone), "" (paper), "" (scissors), "" (lizard) or "" (spock).
                        
- Example. Entrance: [("",""), ("",""), ("","")]. Result: "Player 2".
                        
- You must look for information on how to play with these 5 possibilities.
